Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I book decoration items?

Simply browse our Items page, select the item you want, choose quantity and date, fill in your details, and confirm your booking. It's that easy!

What is your delivery area?

We deliver across Gujarat and nearby states. Delivery charges may vary based on location. Contact us for specific delivery inquiries.

Can I cancel my booking?

Yes, you can cancel pending bookings from your My Bookings page. Confirmed bookings may have cancellation charges. Contact us for assistance.

Do you provide setup services?

Yes! We offer professional setup services for mandap, lighting, and stage decoration. Additional charges apply based on requirements.
